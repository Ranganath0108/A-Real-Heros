A Real Heroes

We all know that cinematic heroes like Batman, IronMan, Spiderman. They save the lives, help the people from dreadful situation and from villians But in Real life, particulary during this pandemic time the real heores were doctors.

Doctors are the immune system of the country. They fight for us to make healthier. In this crucial pandemic stage they played an important role to secure the country.

Hundreds of Doctors Lost their life to serve people. According to IMA covid-registry report, over 800+ doctors sacrificed their lives in second pandemic wave.

That includes over 115 from Bihar,Delhi-109,Uttar Pradesh-79,West Bengal-44,Jhaharkand-39,Andra Pradesh-40 and Southern states like Karnataka recorded 9 deaths,while Kerala & TamilNadu witnessed 24 and 50 deaths respectively.

PM Modi ensured that doctors should be respected and protected

Around 32,063,616* lives were saved by the doctors.It's our duty to follow the SOP's and give respect to the doctors.
